TidyTabs for Chrome

TidyTabs is a Chrome extension powered by our custom machine learning model that declutters your browser in one click. It understands what each tab is about and intelligently groups them by topic — like Work, Entertainment, or Travel Plans — right inside Chrome.

How to Use

Get started with TidyTabs in a few simple steps.

  • 1. Install: Add TidyTabs from the Chrome Web Store. (Official extension link coming soon!)
  • 2. Click: Tap the TidyTabs icon in your Chrome toolbar.
  • 3. Organize: In the popup, click “Organize My Tabs.”
  • 4. Let it work: TidyTabs will read your open tab titles, use our AI model to interpret and group them, and automatically apply Chrome tab groups (like Productivity, News, Entertainment, etc.).
  • Tip: To regroup after opening new tabs, just click the button again!
